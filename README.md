# Steel Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing steel sales performance using Excel-based business intelligence techniques. The objective was to identify sales trends, regional performance, product contribution, customer behavior, and profitability drivers to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze revenue performance across regions and products
* Identify top-performing customer segments
* Evaluate margin contribution and profitability
* Monitor sales trends over time
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Analysis

* Total Revenue
* Total Quantity Sold
* Gross Sales vs Net Sales
* Revenue Trends

### Regional Analysis

* Region-wise Sales
* Country-wise Performance

### Product Analysis

* Product Family Performance
* Product-wise Revenue
* Grade and Specification Analysis

### Customer Analysis

* Customer Segment Contribution
* End Market Analysis

### Profitability Analysis

* Margin Performance
* Gross Margin Comparison
* Freight Cost Impact

### Interactive Filters

* Year
* Region
* Product Family
* Customer Segment
* Sales Representative

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Dashboard Design
* Julius AI
* Business Analytics Techniques

---

## Key Insights

* Identified high-performing regions driving revenue growth.
* Evaluated top-selling steel products and product families.
* Analyzed profitability through margin and gross margin metrics.
* Examined customer segment contribution to overall sales.
* Generated business recommendations for sales optimization.
